<p> Our paper <a href="https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-981-96-2845-2_12"><b>Faster and Improved CD-MAWS with Suffix Automata</b></a> has been accepted at <b>WALCOM 2025</b>. The paper introduces a refined CD-MAWS method for phylogeny estimation, significantly reducing computational complexity using Suffix Automata. </p>
